Share

Tell the world how you felt during your stay at the branch. How was the quality of the services? How was the efficiency of the staff? If you were the manager, what would you do better to make your experience much better?

Review

Tell us what exactly you appreciated about the services you were offered, about the set up of the place, and the staff at large. In addition to that, all tell us exactly what offended you, or what wasn't up to your expectations. And finally, if you have any ideas to suggest to the branch, feel free to add them as well.

Rate

On a scale of 1 to 10, tell us how satisfied you are with:
1. The services and products.
2. The staff that you worked with.
3. The customer feedback you recieved.

Inspiration

There are so many platforms where people share their experiences about the banking sector. Unfortunately, when these posts are made on majority of these platforms, they paint a bad picture of the bank. In addition to that, the different banks do not engage the customers about those experiences for various reasons.

This platform is inspired by situation above. This platform resolves this problem through giving customers an environment to express their issues and also gives the bank a central platform to engage their customers.
